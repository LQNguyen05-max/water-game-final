Drip to the Dunes

Collect as much water as you can by catching the drops in your bucket before the timer runs out!

Instructions

  1. Move the bucket:
    • For Desktop: use the left and right arrow keys
    • For Mobile: swipe left and right
  2. Catch the falling water drops with your bucket to fill the water bar.
  3. You have 60 seconds to fill the water bar as much as possible.
  4. If you fill the water bar before time runs out, you win!
  5. If time runs out and you haven't filled the bar, you lose.
